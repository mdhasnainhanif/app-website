Staff augmentation is ideal if you:
  • Need specialized expertise that your in-house team doesn’t have.
  • Are working on a time-sensitive project with tight deadlines.
  • Want to scale your development team without the long hiring process.
  • Require flexibility to adjust team size as your project evolves.
